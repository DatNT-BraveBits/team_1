### Description

Every year, online fashion retailers lose billions to product returns. The number one reason? "It didn't look like I expected." Customers order multiple sizes, keep one, and send the rest back — costing merchants an average of $10-$15 per return in shipping, restocking, and lost margin. For small and mid-size Shopify stores, this is not just an inconvenience — it is a margin killer.

**MirrorAI: See It On Me** eliminates the guesswork from online shopping. Powered by advanced AI body-mapping and garment-simulation technology, MirrorAI lets your customers see exactly how a product looks on their body type before they click "Add to Cart." The result: fewer returns, higher conversions, and a library of user-generated content that sells for you 24/7.

**How It Works**

Customers upload a photo or use their device camera. MirrorAI instantly generates a realistic virtual try-on image, accurately mapping the garment to their proportions, skin tone, and pose. No green screens. No awkward overlays. Just photorealistic results in under 3 seconds.

**Key Features**

- **Virtual Try-On Engine:** AI-powered garment simulation that works with any product photo. Supports tops, bottoms, dresses, outerwear, and accessories. Customers see themselves wearing your products — not a generic mannequin.
- **Smart Size Advisor:** Combines body measurements with garment-specific fit data to recommend the perfect size. Reduces size-related returns by up to 40%.
- **UGC Gallery:** Customers can save and share their try-on images directly to social media or your on-site gallery. Each share becomes a micro-influencer moment that drives organic traffic back to your store.
- **Merchant Analytics Dashboard:** Track try-on engagement, conversion lift, return rate reduction, and top-performing products. Know exactly which SKUs benefit most from virtual try-on.

**Proven Results**

Merchants using MirrorAI report an average **30% reduction in return rates** within the first 60 days. Conversion rates increase by **25%** on product pages where virtual try-on is enabled. Stores with the UGC Gallery active see a **15% lift in organic traffic** from social shares.

**Seamless Shopify Integration**

MirrorAI installs in under 5 minutes. No theme code editing required. Works with all major Shopify themes and supports Shopify 2.0 app blocks. Fully compatible with Shopify Markets for international stores.

**Pricing That Scales With You**

Free plan available for stores with up to 50 try-ons per month. Paid plans start at $29/month with unlimited try-ons, priority rendering, and full analytics access.

## Demo Live (3 phut)

### Buoc 1: Dashboard
"Day la giao dien MirrorAI trong Shopify Admin. Merchant thay danh sach san pham voi nut 'See It On Me' tren moi san pham."

*Chi vao 3 product cards voi hinh anh*

### Buoc 2: Virtual Try-On
"Gia su mot khach hang muon thu chiec ao T-shirt nay."

*Bam 'See It On Me' tren Classic Fit Cotton T-Shirt*

"Modal mo ra - khach chi can:"
1. *Upload 1 tam anh* - "Keo tha hoac chon anh. Ho tro nguoi, thu cung, bat ky doi tuong nao."
2. *Nhap chieu cao va can nang (tuy chon)* - "De AI tu van size chinh xac hon."
3. *Bam 'Try It On!'*

"AI dang lam viec... GPT-4o phan tich anh khach hang, DALL-E 3 tao anh thu do."

*Doi 15-30 giay, chi vao loading animation*

### Buoc 3: Ket qua
"Day la ket qua! AI da tao anh khach hang mac san pham, dua tren dang nguoi that cua ho."

*Chi vao anh ket qua*

### Buoc 4: Smart Size Advisor
"Ben canh anh, Smart Size Advisor tu dong goi y size dua tren chieu cao, can nang va bang size san pham. Vi du: 'Voi dang cua ban, size M se vua van nhat.'"

### Buoc 5: UGC Save
"Sau khi thu do, khach thay dong 'Love this look?' - ho co the luu anh vao gallery cua shop. Day chinh la UGC tu dong!"

*Bam 'Save to Shop Gallery'*

### Buoc 6: UGC Gallery (Merchant)
"Quay lai dashboard, tab UGC Gallery - merchant thay tat ca anh khach da luu."

*Chuyen sang tab UGC Gallery*

"Merchant co the Approve hoac Reject. Chi anh duoc duyet moi hien tren storefront. Hoan toan kiem soat."

*Bam Approve tren 1 anh*
